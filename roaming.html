Travel + Decency Index

A playful rubric: What felt welcoming? What felt performative? Where did decency show up?

The Method

  • I lead with curiosity, not conclusions.
  • I talk to people who aren't trying to sell me anything.
  • I write down what surprised me.
  • I look for patterns: who gets included, who gets ignored, what behavior gets rewarded.
  • I'm not here to dunk on places. I'm here to understand them.
